Front-end development

Rwanda Education Commons

A portal that enables Rwandan teachers to collaborate and access resources. A second iteration was launched in early 2011, and is based on usability research completed in Rwanda in June 2010 and in New York in October 2010. Check out the Rwanda Education Commons.

NGOConnect.net

A document repository dedicated to connecting and strengthening non-governmental organizations, networks and support organizations worldwide. Built on Liferay Portal 5.0.

GLP main site

Redesigned August 2009. New features include dynamic account information in top bar, and integrated Google Custom Search. Built on Liferay Portal 5.2.2.
