Sketchnotes

Practicing sketchnoting allows us to discover improvements in our working memory, visual expression, and even own speaking technique. If looking at sketchnotes makes your brain light up, I encourage you to give sketchnoting a try the next time you attend a presentation. If you survive, give it another try, and another, and see what you gain over time.
